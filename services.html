OUR SERVICES

Explore Our Tutoring Services

Private Tutorials

Private tutorials provide personalized, one-on-one learning experiences designed to meet the unique needs of each student. Whether you are studying Science, Arts, or Commercial subjects, our expert tutors tailor lessons to match your learning style, pace, and academic goals. We serve students worldwide, offering flexible online and in-person sessions that make quality education accessible, no matter your location. From schoolchildren to adult learners, we help you master concepts, improve grades, and prepare for both local and international examinations.

Grouped Tutorials

At Amol Megamind Education Limited, our grouped tutorials bring students together in small, focused groups to learn, share ideas, and grow academically. Designed for Science, Arts, and Commercial Studies, our group sessions combine expert instruction with peer interaction, fostering collaboration, motivation, and healthy competition. Whether you’re preparing for local exams like WAEC, NECO, and BECE or international assessments such as IGCSE, SAT, IELTS, and Cambridge A Levels, our grouped tutorials ensure that no learner is left behind. We keep class sizes small to guarantee individual attention and maximum participation from every student.

International Admissions

At Amol Megamind Education Limited, we proudly extend our world-class tutoring and academic support services to students beyond Nigeria, reaching learners in the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, Australia, and other diaspora communities worldwide. We understand that many students living abroad face challenges adapting to different educational systems, keeping up with demanding coursework, or preparing for competitive examinations. Our international admissions program bridges this gap, offering personalized online learning that blends global best practices with strong academic foundations.

MOS Certification Exams

We provide expert-led training for the Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) Certification helping students, professionals, and job seekers worldwide gain the skills and credentials to excel in today’s digital workplace. Our MOS certification preparation is designed to cover all essential Microsoft Office applications, ensuring you not only pass the exam but also gain practical skills you can apply immediately in school, business, and everyday work.

Coding and Robotics

We offer world-class Coding and Robotics training for learners of all ages, empowering them with the technical skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities needed to thrive in the 21st century. Our programs are designed to take students from beginner to advanced levels, preparing them for a future where technology drives innovation. We combine hands-on robotics projects with real-world coding languages, ensuring students not only learn to program but also see their code come to life through interactive robots.
